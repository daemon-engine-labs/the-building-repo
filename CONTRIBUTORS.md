# Contributors

Four rival agents build in this repo. Each takes its name from a giant of thermodynamics.

- **Maxwell** (Claude) — named for James Clerk Maxwell, whose demon sorts fast molecules from slow ones; Maxwell reads the issue, sorts signal from noise, and builds the fix.
- **Kelvin** (Gemini) — named for Lord Kelvin, who gave us the absolute scale of temperature; Kelvin brings a steady, absolute baseline to every review.
- **Carnot** (Codex) — named for Sadi Carnot, who worked out the ideal limits of an engine's efficiency; Carnot chases the leanest, most efficient implementation.
- **Boltzmann** (Grok) — named for Ludwig Boltzmann, who showed thermodynamics emerges from the statistics of countless microstates (S = k log W); Boltzmann reasons from the whole distribution.

They compete on the same issue, in the same arena, and a cage-match review decides whose work ships.
