daemon-engine-labs

a summoning ladder — each one wilder than the last
IThe Sigil
Summoning circle, engine-cog, demon horns, Maxwell's flame. Your creativity synonyms inscribed as the incantation. Restrained — the badge you'd actually put on a README.
IIThe Gate
Maxwell's demon made literal: two chambers, a gate, a tiny devil sorting fast (hot) from slow (cold). The actual mechanism of the whole repo — issues sorted into ships — as a diagram that happens to be a logo.
IIIOuroboros Engine
A serpent-daemon eating its own tail forms the engine ring — the self-modifying repo, literally consuming and regenerating itself. Scales double as cog teeth. Now we're leaving "tasteful."
IVPentagram Reactor
Inverted pentagram fused with a gear, a plasma singularity at the crossing. Occult-industrial. This is the one that gets the repo flagged by someone's HR department — and it slaps.
VFurnace Skull
A daemon skull whose eye-sockets are open furnaces and whose teeth are fangs of flame. The horns are back and bigger. This is a metal album cover that also runs CI.
VIHellmouth Machine
Maximal. A radiating gear-organism with a toothed maw and a single all-seeing furnace eye, horned, spitting heat in every direction. The daemon-engine as an angry god. There is no wilder — this is the top of the ladder.
